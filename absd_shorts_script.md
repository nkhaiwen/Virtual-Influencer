### [0:05 - 0:15] Explanation of ABSD
**Scene**: Wife gestures with the blueprint, pointing to sections. Camera zooms in on her face for emphasis.

**Voiceover**: "ABSD stands for Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty—it's an extra tax in Singapore on top of the regular stamp duty when you buy a property. It's designed to cool the property market and make housing more affordable for first-time buyers."

**Visuals**: Animated graphics: Icons of house + tax calculator. Text: "Extra tax on property purchases".

### [0:15 - 0:25] Who Pays and Rates
**Scene**: Wife picks up a measuring tape (renovation tool prop), measuring an imaginary space on the blueprint.

**Voiceover**: "Who pays it? The buyer! Rates vary: Singaporeans might pay 0% for their first home, but up to 12% for a second property. Permanent Residents and foreigners face higher rates, like 5-15% or more, depending on the property type."

**Visuals**: Infographic: Buyer types (Singaporean, PR, Foreigner) with rate ranges. Keep it simple, not overwhelming.

### [0:25 - 0:35] Tips and Advice
**Scene**: Wife stands up, walks to a window with plants, looking optimistic.

**Voiceover**: "To navigate ABSD, plan your finances early, consult a property agent or financial advisor, and factor it into your budget. Remember, it's just one part of building your dream home!"

**Visuals**: Quick tips text: "Plan ahead 💡 Consult pros 🤝 Budget wisely 💰". B-roll: Her smiling, holding a calculator app on phone.

**Scene**: Wife sits back down, crosses legs comfortably, ends with a thumbs-up.

**Voiceover**: "Stay positive, nest builders! Your perfect space is worth it. Like and follow for more home tips. #BTOJourney #ABSDExplained"
