# The Legacy Architects: Character Profiles (Dad & Mom)

This summary outlines the specifications for two virtual influencer characters representing Singapore's active senior demographic, as detailed in the provided character profile screenshots.

## 1. Core Philosophy & Demographics
- **Target Audience:** Adults aged 55–75 (Merdeka and Pioneer Generations).
- **Key Concept:** **Active Ageing** and the **"Un-Retirement"** mindset. Retirement is viewed as a "rewiring" of purpose, focusing on exploration, digital literacy, and health.
- **Tone:** Rejecting tropes of frailty; characters are depicted as active, engaged, and vital.

## 2. Character Archetypes

### **Dad (The Steward)**
- **Role:** Strategic Planner and Provider.
- **Motivations:** Financial security, rightsizing for equity, and legacy building.
- **Interests:** Community gardening, brisk walking, and strategic asset management.

### **Mom (The Matriarch)**
- **Role:** Legacy Keeper and Nurturer.
- **Motivations:** Family harmony, intergenerational wealth transfer, and emotional stewardship.
- **Interests:** Caregiving, travel, and maintaining cultural rituals.

## 3. Visual Design & Style ("Active Silver")
- **Keywords:** Dignity, Vitality, Stewardship.
- **Dad’s Look:** "Smart Active" – High-performance polo shirts, tailored chinos or bermudas, high-tech walking shoes (e.g., Skechers), and a health-monitoring smartwatch.
- **Mom’s Look:** "Modern Matriarch" – Elegant, breathable natural fabrics (linen/cotton) with modern cuts.
- **Cultural Signifiers:**
    - **Jade Bangle:** A critical marker for the Mother, rendered in "Imperial Green" to symbolize purity, wealth, and heirloom value.
    - **Gold Jewelry:** Layered gold chains reflecting traditional preferences for gold as a symbol of prosperity.
- **Grooming:** Natural silver or grey hair promoted as a "badge of wisdom" (Dad: neat trim; Mom: fashionable bob).
- **Posture:** Upright and engaged, typically shown in motion (e.g., Tai Chi, park connector walks).

## 4. Financial & Narrative Context
- **Rightsizing Dilemma:** Addressing the emotional and logistical tension of moving from a family home to a smaller unit to unlock property equity.
- **Legacy Planning:** Emphasizing efficient wealth transfer through insurance and trusts.
- **Cultural Rituals:** Integration of local customs, such as the "Pineapple Rolling" ceremony for new homes.
- **Linguistic Style:** Use of Singaporean vernacular (e.g., *Huat Ah*, *Pang Gang*, *Makan*) in dialogue.
